Intended schedule for the 2020 festival

May 22-25, 2020

Friday, May 22
BALLROOM BANQUET HALL CAFE and OFF-SITE
5:30-6:45 pm: Circular Movements around a Common Axis - Maja & Marko (Int/Adv) 5:30-6:45 pm: Touchstones for Musicality in Tango (Part 1) - Cadencia, Phrasing, Accents - Octavio & Carla (Int & Up)
7-8:15 pm: IN-DEPTH CLOSE EMBRACE SEMINAR (1st of 4) - Couples Technique 1 - Presence and Connection - Maja & Marko (Partners-only) 7-8:15 pm: Beginner Track - Workshop 1 - "It Takes Two to Tango" - Gustavo, Kristin & Amy (Beginner)  
  8:30-9:45 pm: Pleasing Combinations for Vals - Fabian & Lola (Int & Up) 8:30-9:30 pm: FREE & FUN Line-of-Dance Training Workshop. The festival is more fun when everyone knows and follows the codes of the dance floor!
9:30 pm-3 am: Milonga with LIVE MUSIC by Emiliano Messiez (piano & direction), Javier Sanchez (bandoneon), Christine Brebes (violin) and Pablo Lanouguere (bass). DJ Gustavo Rember. Performances: Ronda de los maestros, Adam & Ciko, Octavio & Carla.    

Saturday, May 23
BALLROOM BANQUET HALL CAFE and OFF-SITE
12:15-1:30 pm: Follower's Technique - Carla (All Levels) 12:15-1:30 pm: Leader's Technique - Octavio & Matias (All Levels)  
1:45-3 pm: Half Turns - Matias's favorite figure, not because they are easy but because of how readily they allow us to maximize our creativity on the dance floor! - Matias (Int & Up) 1:45-3 pm: LA PROPUESTA SEMINAR (1st of 3) - Intensive Training in Analyzing Tango Structure in order to move toward greater freedom in improvisation - Octavio & Carla. Partners-only.  
3:15-4:30 pm: IN-DEPTH CLOSE EMBRACE SEMINAR (2nd of 4) - Walking and/in the Close Embrace - Maja & Marko (Partners-only) 3:15-4:45 pm: Beginner Track - Workshop 2 - Myself, My Partner & The Dance Floor - Gustavo, Kristin & Amy (Beginner)  
4:45-6 pm: Every Aspect of Sacadas for Men & Women - Fabian & Lola (Int & Up) 5-6:15 pm: TANGO HISTORY, MUSIC & DANCE SEMINAR (1st of 3) - From tango's origins through Julio De Caro - Gustavo, Leandro & Emiliano (All levels)
  6:30-7:45 pm: ON & OFF AXIS SEMINAR (1st of 3) - How to adapt your embrace to manage the axis - Fabian & Lola (Partners-only)  
  8-9:15 pm: Painting the floor - Navigation is the key to a successful dance. In this class we will learn how to strategize our dance based on where we can go in the space. - Adam & Ciko. (All levels) 8:30-9:30 pm: Icebreaker Social Hour. Meet more people! Get more dances!
9:30 pm-3 am: Milonga with LIVE MUSIC by the Típica Messiez. DJ Octavio Fernandez. Performances: Maja & Marko and Fabian & Lola. 10 pm-2 am: Overflow Room - Mix of New & Old Tango Music - DJ Manuk Colakyan.  
    3-7 am: After-Party at the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School (2030 Frankford Ave, Phila, PA 19125), with DJ Micaela.

Sunday, May 24
BALLROOM BANQUET HALL CAFE and OFF-SITE
12:15-1:30 pm: Follower's Technique - Maja (All levels). **NOTE:** Please plan to take this class in the type of clothes you usually wear for the milonga. The same heel height, for example, and the same kind of clothing you typically wear when you go out dancing. Maja will explain in the workshop why this is so important. 12:15-1:30 pm: ON & OFF AXIS SEMINAR (2nd of 3) - Fundamentals of out-of-axis - Fabian & Lola (Partners-only)  
1:45-3 pm: Different Types of Ganchos - Fabian & Lola (Int/Adv) 1:45-3 pm: LA PROPUESTA SEMINAR (2nd of 3) - Intensive Training in Analyzing Tango Structure in order to move toward greater freedom in improvisation - Octavio & Carla (Partners-only)  
3:15-4:30 pm: IN-DEPTH CLOSE EMBRACE SEMINAR (3rd of 4) - Couples Technique 2: Rolling Close Embrace - Maja & Marko (Partners-only) 3:15-4:30 pm: Dynamic Changes - Techniques for varying the dynamic in turns and in linear movements - Matias (Int)
4:45-6 pm: Milonga Lisa vs. Milonga con Traspié - Maja & Marko (Int/Adv) 4:45-6 pm: How To Dance Piazzolla (Part 1) - Andres & Meredith (Int/Adv)  
6:15-7:30 pm: Soltadas - Exploring the fundamentals of Soltadas, inc. how to keep the connection and how to improvise with them too! - Matias (Int/Adv) 6:15-7:30 pm: TANGO HISTORY, MUSIC & DANCE SEMINAR (2nd of 3) - From the 1930s through the Golden Age - Gustavo, Leandro & Emiliano (All levels)  
7:45-9 pm: Tanguísimo - Cortes, Quebradas, Paradas. These important movements from tango history are not used enough today. Incorporating them in your dance increases your range of musical expression, and gives your dance a super-tanguero look and feel. - Octavio & Carla. (Int/Adv) 7:45-9:15 pm: Beginner Track - Workshop 3 - Myself, My Partner, The Dance Floor, and The Music - Gustavo, Kristin & Amy (Beginner)  
9:30 pm-3 am: Milonga with LIVE MUSIC by the Típica Messiez. DJ Marcelo Rosensaft. Performances: Andres & Meredith, Matias & Carla. 10 pm-2 am: Overflow Room - Mix of Old & New Tango Music. DJ Eli Ambron.  
    3-7 am: After-Party at the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School (2030 Frankford Ave, Phila, PA 19125), with DJ Alberto.

Monday, May 25 (Memorial Day)
BALLROOM BANQUET HALL CAFE and OFF-SITE
12-1:30 pm: Beginner Track - Workshop 4 - Adding Spice to Simple Moves - Gustavo, Kristin & Amy (Beginner) 12:15-1:30 pm: Back Sacadas for Both - Disassociation, Technique, Structure, Creativity - Matias (Int/Adv)  
1:45-3 pm: ON & OFF AXIS SEMINAR (3rd of 3) - Combining elements out-of-axis - Fabian & Lola (Partners-only) 1:45-3 pm: Touchstones for Musicality in Tango (Part 2) - How to Dance to Different Orchestras, focusing on Di Sarli, Pugliese & D'Arienzo - Octavio & Carla (Int/Adv)  
3:15-4:30 pm: IN-DEPTH CLOSE EMBRACE SEMINAR (4th of 4) - Maja & Marko - Playing with the embrace (Partners-only) 3:15-4:30 pm: How To Dance Piazzolla (Part 2) - Andres & Meredith (Int/Adv)  
4:45-6 pm: Technique for Walking - "Bailar a Tierra" - Developing a ground-heavy tanguero walk. We are going to discover how the way we walk can help us to free our upper body - Matias (All Levels) 4:45-6 pm: LA PROPUESTA SEMINAR (3rd of 3) - Intensive Training in Analyzing Tango Structure in order to move toward greater freedom in improvisation - Octavio & Carla (Partners-Only)
6:15-7:30 pm: The Tortoise and the Hare - By creating different textures we can build energy and release it into really amazing places - Adam & Ciko (Int/Adv) 6:15-7:30 pm: TANGO HISTORY, MUSIC & DANCE SEMINAR (3rd of 3) - Tango after the Golden Age through today - Gustavo, Leandro & Emiliano (All levels)  
8:30 pm-2 am: Milonga with LIVE MUSIC by Emiliano Messiez (piano & direction), Leandro Ragusa, (bandoneon), Christine Brebes (violin) and Pablo Lanouguere (bass). DJs Adam & Ciko. Performances: Alberto & Micaela, Amy & Kristin, Ronda de los Maestros.    
GO HOME HAPPY! :)

Seeking Volunteers!

It takes the combined efforts of over 60 staff and volunteers to make the festival run smoothly. Please consider joining the volunteer team! Thank you in advance!

Volunteer Compensation

0-5 hours: Earns our undying gratitude 6 hours: Earns our undying gratitude, plus a milonga pass 8 hours: Earns our undying gratitude, plus a milonga pass and two workshops 10 hours: Earns our undying gratitude, plus a milonga pass and four workshops 14 hours: Earns our undying gratitude, plus a milonga pass and six workshops 18 hours: Earns our undying gratitude, plus a milonga pass and nine workshops

Volunteer Special

If you volunteer to support the After-Party milongas, which may mean setting up, cleaning up and/or working during the after-parties, your hours worked count as double.
