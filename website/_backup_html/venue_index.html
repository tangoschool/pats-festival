Venue

Lithuanian Music Hall Association

All events are held at the Lithuanian Music Hall.

2715 E Allegheny Ave Philadelphia, PA 19134 (map)

We are in love with our venue and hope to hold the festival there forever.

The Lithuanian Music Hall offers:

  • A gorgeous 5,500 square-foot ballroom, where we hold large workshops, and the nightly milongas
  • A large banquet hall, where workshops are held
  • A foyer that we transform into a tango boutique, with shoes and clothing
  • A cafe space

This venue has a liquor license and will be providing a cash bar at all milongas, so it is not ok to BYO at this venue. Thank you!

Parking Valet parking for the evening / night events. During the day, please use street parking.
