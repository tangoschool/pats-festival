Concerts

We are thrilled to offer four seated concerts followed by four live music milongas at this year's festival.

Friday, May 22, 8 pm

Greg Dimit Memorial Concert

Juan Villarreal, guitar and voice Patricio Crom, guitar

This year, we lost a beloved community member, someone whose generosity and passion for new tango music were instrumental in bringing many projects and events into being over the past five years. This concert is presented in memory of Greg Dimit.

Among other repertoire, Juan & Patricio will perform music from three albums that were supported by generous contributions from Greg & Karen Dimit: D'Arienzo en Guitarras, Moderno Trovador, and D'Arienzo en Guitarras II, to be released in 2026.

Saturday, May 23, 8 pm

New Album Presentation

Emiliano Messiez, Piano Juan Villarreal, Voice

Pianist Emiliano Messiez & Vocalist Juan Villarreal will present their new album, produced by Bochinche Records with generous support from Hanna Augustynowicz and Reiner Ams.

Sunday, May 24, 8 pm

Urban Tangos

Mariano Castro, Piano

Mariano Castro will present his new album, which he recorded at the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School in January 2026, on our beloved Yamaha grand piano.

Monday, May 25, 8 pm

Gran Peña Folklorica

Juan Villarreal, Guitar & Vocals Patricio Crom, Guitar Emiliano Messiez, Piano Sergio Reyes, Violin Amelia Foss, Dancer
