Parking

This year, we are happy to be able to offer self-parking in a nearby business's lot for most (but not all) festival hours. A security person will be in the lot during all permitted parking hours.

The parking location will be emailed to those who pay for parking.

Permitted Parking Lot Hours:

Friday, May 22: 5:30 PM - 3:30 AM Saturday, May 23: 1:00 PM - 4:30 AM Sunday, May 24: 11:30 AM - 3:30 AM Monday, May 25: 11:30 AM - 3 AM

PLEASE do not park in the lot outside of these hours as it may mean we lose all access to the lot.

For now, we are only selling "full festival parking," meaning during all permitted hours. The cost is $120 per car.

Now that pre-registration has closed, the best way to pay for parking is to venmo Meredith.
