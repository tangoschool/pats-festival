Lithuanian Community Special Promotion

The Philadelphia Tango Festival is extremely grateful to the Lithuanian Cultural Center and the Lithuanian Community in Philadelphia for hosting our yearly event. Since 2017, the collaboration between the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School and the Lithuanian Music Hall has been mutually beneficial and very successful, involving the tango festival, as well as many other events.

We'd love to involve more of the LCC community in the 2025 Philadelphia Tango Festival. No experience is necessary, and no partner is required.

A Special Invitation

Members of the Lithuanian Cultural Center community are welcome to use the code "LCC" to access 20% off any festival registration.

What We Recommend

Please consider registering for the festival BEGINNER PASS. It is the best deal of the festival, and includes a total of 20 activities:

  • Four beginner-level Argentine tango dance classes (the "beginner track"). This year, the beginner track will be taught by Festival Director Meredith Klein, with her partner Ignacio Ondartz.
  • All 4 milongas (nightly social dances), including tango dance performances and amazing live music
  • All 4 practicas (less formal social dances for practicing)
  • Four lectures on the festival theme, the music and life of Anibal Troilo, including two with live music demonstrations
  • One seated concert
  • Three dance workshops on Argentine tango technique, exercises and technique for individuals to get ready to dance with a partner

Check out the Complete Festival Schedule to see dates and times for each activity.

We will provide special seating at festival milongas (social dances with live music and dance performances) for LCC community members, and also a special reception only for the LCC Community.

If you have questions, you are welcome to contact Vytas Maciunas (215-739-4831) or you can register directly for the Beginner Pass or any festival offering, with the discount code "LCC" (20% off).
