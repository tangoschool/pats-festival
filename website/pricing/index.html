Pricing & Passes

__Update: Pre-registration is now closed.__ However, you are welcome to purchase any pass or individual event at the door, with the sole exception of the Saturday Milonga. You can still attend the Saturday Milonga by purchasing a pass that it is part of OR purchasing another pass, like the Concerts & Lectures Pass, and adding a Saturday Milonga ticket. Check out the [at-the-door prices for passes and tickets](/pricing). __For registrants:__ Please check out the [FESTIVAL FULL SCHEDULE](https://drive.google.com/file/d/19lrZBaO0DbpmR9MdziF2zYufpltoQfGl/view?usp=drive_link) for the dates and times of your events. If you purchased a pass, the festival schedule also helps you understand which activities are included in that pass.
