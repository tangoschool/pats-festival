Cafe ZITA

Last year, the festival experienced an enormous upgrade, when La Luli took over the Cafe. We are so fortunate to have her back in 2026.

She'll be offering a variety of delicious and healthy meals, snacks and drinks to keep you going during ♾️ hours of dancing!

CAFE HOURS

Cafe Zita will offer extended hours this year, so you can truly eat breakfast, lunch and dinner at the festival venue!

Friday, May 22, 1-10 pm
Saturday, May 23, 11:30 am-10 pm
Sunday, May 24, 11:30 am-10 pm
Monday, May 25, 11:30 am-9 pm

☕ Hot Beverages

• Americano • Cortado (Espresso with a touch of milk) • Café con Leche (Espresso with more milk – similar to a latte, plant-based milk available) • Black Tea / Herbal Infusions

• Iced Coffee • Iced Tea • Water of the Day • Smoothies

• Alfajor de Maicena (cornstarch cookie with dulce de leche) • Chocolate Alfajor with Dulce de Leche • Medialuna (Argentinian croissant) • Cake Slice - • Chipa (savory cheesy bread – gluten-free) • Flan with dulce de leche

🥟 Savory Dishes

Tarts & Quiches • Bacon Quiche • Mushroom Quiche • Mixed Vegetable Tart - (vegan, gluten-free) Empanadas • Knife-Cut Beef • Chicken & Green Onion • Humita (corn, onion, béchamel sauce) • Spinach & Cheese
