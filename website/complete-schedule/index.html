6:15-7:45 pm: LECTURE / CONVERSATION - TANGO & GENDER: A NECESSARY DEBATE In this talk, tango and lunfardo scholar Matías Mauricio proposes looking at the history of tango with fresh eyes: asking how its masculine imagery was constructed, what place women were given—and denied—and what ideas we continue to repeat without realizing it. With a close and critical approach, he reviews stereotypes, misunderstandings, and romantic views that no longer serve us. The proposal is to rethink tango from its dance, music, and lyrics, to make way for a broader, more diverse, and more current perspective. - Matias. Passes: ALL, EBS, Friday, BegPass, MCPL+, C+L
