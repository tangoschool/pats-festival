Pricing & Passes Last Minute 2024

Pre-registration for the 2024 Philadelphia Tango Festival is now closed.

However, you can purchase passes at the door, and most single activities. Due to fire capacity restrictions, we cannot accommodate walk-ins to milongas, except by people with a festival pass of some kind. If you buy a pass at the door, you can still access single milongas.

Passes available at the door:

All Access Pass: $700 Almost Everything Pass: $525 Four-Class MOLINETE Seminar: $190 Duelo Criollo (TM) Seminar with B & R: $190 How Do You Write A Tango? Seminar: $140 Milongas, Practicas, Concerts & Lectures Pass: $360 Milongas & Practicas Pass: $240 Concerts Pass: $125 Lectures Pass: $90 Beginner Pass: $200 Beginner Track: $110 Friday Pass: $170 Saturday Pass: $240 Sunday Pass: $240 Monday Pass: $240

Anyone may purchase any of these single activities at the door, as well:

Single Workshop (excludes Molinete & Duello Criollo seminars): $50 Single Chacarera Workshop: $40 Single Beginner workshop: $40 Single Practica: $25 Single Concert: $45 Single Lecture: $35 Yoga Class (Free for anyone with any pass): $20 Talk about TTP (Free to anyone with any pass) Mate Hour (Free for anyone with any pass) Access to Cafe (Any festival participant is welcome)

Anyone with a festival pass can add single milongas for $60 each, but unfortunately, we cannot accommodate single milonga walk-ins from people without pass, due to fire capacity restrictions.
